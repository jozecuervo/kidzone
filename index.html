The idea

Small projects with real product constraints

Kidzone is an evolving open-source playground by José. Each project is deliberately self-contained, easy to inspect, and explicit about safety, storage, network access, and the help a young player may need.

  • Local firstNo accounts, chat, analytics, or surprise uploads.
  • Made to exploreCreation, puzzles, and experiments over attention traps.
  • Built in the openPlain web technology, documented metadata, and automated checks.
